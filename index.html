Welcome to the High Energy Physics Group

Our research group at the University of Pennsylvania focuses on exploring fundamental questions in particle physics. We work at the Large Hadron Collider to search for new physics beyond the Standard Model, with a special focus on trigger systems and applications of AI and ML. Our team is passionate about advancing our understanding of the universe at the highest energies.
